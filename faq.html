SeniorNest — Help & FAQ

Senior care directory & coordination

About SeniorNest

What is SeniorNest?
A directory of senior care providers in Ontario, plus tools to coordinate a loved one’s care — medications, appointments, visits and a shared journal.
How do reminders work?
The app shows medications due, refills coming up and upcoming appointments. With Premium you can also receive email or SMS reminders.
What is the shared contacts list?
A Premium feature to keep pharmacy, GP, specialist and emergency contacts in one place for the whole care team.

Plans & account

Is it free to use?
Yes. Browsing the directory and one profile with the basic coordination tools — records, appointments, schedule, journal and calendar — are free. Premium is $15/month CAD and unlocks more.
What do I get with Premium?
Multiple profiles, family sharing, email/SMS notifications, a weekly newsletter, document storage, export/print, and the platform’s standout features.
How do I upgrade or cancel?
Upgrade any time from the Plans page. You can cancel whenever you like — premium features stay active until the end of your billing period.

Sharing, privacy & security

Who can see my information?
Only you and the people you choose to invite. No other member or family can see your data.
How does family sharing work?
Premium lets you invite people by email and give each one a role — admin, editor, or viewer. When they sign in, they see exactly what their role allows.
Is my information secure?
Yes. Each profile’s data is private to you and the people you invite, and access is enforced per account.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data stays in your account. You simply lose access to the premium features until you upgrade again.

For businesses

Can businesses get listed in the directory?
Yes — we welcome local providers. Get in touch through our contact page to be featured.
